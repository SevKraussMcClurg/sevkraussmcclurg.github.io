About Sev Krauss-McClurg

Sev is an experienced prop designer, stage manager, and crew member. He has worked on over 15 shows across 7 years of experience. He is graduating from Eastside Preparatory School and will be pursuing a BFA in Technical Direction from the University of Southern California next fall. He is a passionate theatrical artisan and a strong organizer in all aspects. He is seeking to learn everything available about theatrical design and production.

Sev enjoys listening to musicals in his free time and discovering new favorites. Currently he listens to a few on repeat: Operation Mincemeat, Chess, Catch Me If You Can, and The Producers.
