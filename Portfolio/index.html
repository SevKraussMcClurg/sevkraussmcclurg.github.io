Recent Works

Fiddler on the Roof

Properties Manager - 2024

Fiddler on the Roof is a musical about a Russian town in 1905 where a Jewish Orthodox community has to adjust to the changing world around them and a father who has to accept his daughters' love interests. Directed by Kyle Gerstel at Village Theater KIDSTAGE.

The Addams Family

Properties Manager / Assistant Stage Manager - 2024

The Addams Family is a fun musical full of jokes and puns about a death-filled family learning to accept who their daughter is. Directed by Ian Duncan at Eastside Preparatory School.

Radium Girls

Stage Manager - 2024

Radium Girls, written by D.W. Gregory, is a compelling drama about the true story of the women who worked at the Radium plant owned by the US Radium Corporation in New Jersey. Directed by Ian Duncan at Eastside Preparatory School.
