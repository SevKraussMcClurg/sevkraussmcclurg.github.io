The Addams Family

The Addams Family was directed by Ian Duncan at Eastside Preparatory School in spring 2024. For this show, I worked as the properties manager and assistant stage manager. As properties manager, I facilitated the design and construction of a variety of props within the show, for example, this apple to ther right.

This apple needed to break in half after getting 'shot' by an arrow onstage. I achieved this by taking a fake styrofoam apple, cutting it in half, sealing the two sides with air dry clay, painting them, and adding magnets. This allowed the apple to snap shut and stay shut during the scene until an actor pulled it apart.

There were a lot of challenges with this show, for example, a song where 12 actors each brought on a prop that corresponded to a lyric in quick succession. I had to both construct the props as the properties manager and then figure out how they were going to get across the stage and be stored as the assistant stage manager. Both were solved with input from the director and careful knowledge of the limitations of the backstage space.
